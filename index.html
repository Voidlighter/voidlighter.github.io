I chose the name Voidlighter for a couple reasons: For one, “Cade Voidlighter” has a fun ring to it, like a “Luke Skywalker” or something, and I think that's pretty neat. The main reason, though, is more personal to me: I think sometimes as we make our way through life, it looks like an empty void, devoid of hope or trajectory. Even when we know this isn't true, it isn't always easy to remember that. Friends are essential to light our path, to show the “void” for what it is. The void of space may appear empty, but it's really just dark. In the light, we might not feel so alone or afraid.

I want to bring together a positive community of people to spread hope, and light the void. And you're invited.
