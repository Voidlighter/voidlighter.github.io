Time for some gaming, wholesome fun, a little learning here and there, and we'll figure the rest out as we go.

I chose the name Voidlighter for a couple reasons: For one, “Cade Voidlighter” has a fun ring to it, like a “Luke Skywalker” or something, and I think that's pretty neat. The main reason, though, is more personal to me: I think sometimes as we make our way through life, it looks like an empty void, devoid of hope or trajectory. Even when we *know* this isn't true, it isn't always easy to remember that. Friends are essential to light our path, to show the “void” for what it is. The void of space may appear empty, but it's really just dark. In the light, we might not feel so alone or afraid. I want to bring together a positive community of people to spread hope, and light the void. And you're invited.

About Us

For years, Josh wanted to run a narrative-driven RPG campaign that was grounded, connected and thought-provoking. Eventually, he realized he would have to design that game himself. He and his team are excited to bring you games that will change the way you show up at the table.

What we’re working on

Plugsuit

Plugsuit is a game about giant robots, dark secrets and terrible consequences, inspired by Neon Genesis Evangelion and the SCP Foundation Wiki. It's also a game about resilience in the face of trauma. Plugsuit is Powered By FATE, and uses Dread-style tower mechanics. Coming soon to itch.io.
